Hello, I'm Rajeev

I'm a traveller, food lover and passionate observer of life's small and large moments. This blog is my space to share the things the places I've visited, the food I've devoured, and the ideas that keep me up at night.

I grew up in India and have spent years chasing experiences that push me out of my comfort zone — India offers a vast landscape of spiritual depth and culinary variety that can push any traveler’s boundaries, from the high-altitude monasteries of the North to the ancient island temples of the South, finding the best street food.

I write because it helps me think. I share because sometimes our most private thoughts turn out to be the most universal ones. If even one thing I write makes you feel a little less alone, or inspires you to book that trip, try that dish, or start that journal — this blog has done its job.
